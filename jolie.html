Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt’s Son Pax Jolie-Pitt Suffers Head Injury in Bike Crash

Pax Jolie-Pitt, the son of Hollywood actors Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, reportedly suffered a head injury following a bike crash over the weekend. The incident occurred while the 19-year-old was riding his bicycle in Los Angeles.

According to sources close to the family, Pax was enjoying a casual ride when he lost control of his bike and took a hard fall. Bystanders quickly came to his aid and called emergency services. Pax was transported to a nearby hospital where he was treated for his injuries.

The severity of the injury has not been disclosed, but it is understood that Pax is currently recovering at home. “It was a scary moment for everyone,” an insider said. “Angelina and Brad were both informed immediately and have been by his side through the entire ordeal.”

Pax, who has largely stayed out of the public eye despite his famous parents, is known for his passion for the arts and photography. The family has asked for privacy as they focus on Pax's recovery.

The incident has sparked conversations about bike safety, particularly in busy urban areas like Los Angeles. Experts recommend always wearing a helmet, following traffic rules, and being mindful of road conditions to prevent accidents.

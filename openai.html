OpenAI Starts Roll-out of Advanced Voice Mode to Some ChatGPT Plus Users

OpenAI has begun rolling out an advanced voice mode feature to a select group of ChatGPT Plus users, providing an enhanced, more interactive experience with the AI. This new feature allows users to engage in real-time conversations with the AI using natural speech, significantly improving accessibility and usability.

The advanced voice mode is part of OpenAI's ongoing efforts to make AI interactions more intuitive and human-like. Users can now ask questions, give commands, or engage in dialogue by simply speaking, with the AI responding in a conversational tone.

According to OpenAI, the feature is currently being tested with a limited number of users, with plans to expand access in the coming months. Early feedback suggests that the voice mode is well-received, offering a new dimension to how users interact with ChatGPT.

“This is just the beginning,” an OpenAI spokesperson said. “We’re excited to see how users will utilize the voice mode, and we’re committed to refining the experience based on their feedback.”

For now, the voice mode is available exclusively to ChatGPT Plus subscribers, a premium tier that offers faster response times and priority access to new features. OpenAI has yet to announce when the feature will be available to all users.
